THE FOUNDERS

Dick and Darlene Muth

In 1970, a young couple living in Ethan, South Dakota had a vision to provide electrical services to everyone in a safe, timely and professional manner. That young couple was Dick and Darlene Muth. Now, decades later, their hard work and commitment to Muth Electric hasn’t changed, and it’s grown into one of the largest electrical contractors in the region with over 500 employees—all dedicated to the same mission established by Dick and Darlene.
