About Us

Muth Electric was founded in 1970 by Dick and Darlene Muth, a young couple just beginning their lives together in Ethan, South Dakota. Since then, the company has grown into one of the largest electrical contractors in the region with over 500 employees. Still proud to be family owned, Muth Electric continues its positive growth into the future by following the mission established by Dick and Darlene.

The Muth Difference

Our mission at Muth Electric is to focus on the customer as the most important asset of our company by providing exceptional value, while encouraging leadership and a friendly and helpful spirit among all employees. We will accomplish this mission through a team commitment to professionalism, fairness and honesty, quality workmanship, exceptional service, and communication.
